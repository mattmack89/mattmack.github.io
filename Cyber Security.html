State of The Art Cyber Security Services

We Understand The Impact of Security!

At Wider Security, we understand how crucial high-impact security is for you. With today’s technology, security breaches are more of a concern today than ever before. We know these security risks and work to help your organization secure your networks and information from outside sources. We understand the importance of creating secure networks.

Assess & Discover

We start with an assessment of your needs. We must distinguish which assets are the most valuable to you and how best to protect them from any potential breaches. Whether you have no cyber security protocols or currently have a system in place, we will need to probe your networks for any vulnerabilities and inherent weaknesses. As experts in the cyber security industry, we will simulate attacks to determine where you need to strengthen your security.

This includes:

  • A thorough tactical plan
  • Risk ranked vulnerabilities
  • Penetration testing & network vulnerability discovery

Review Your Cyber Risk

Finding these weaknesses or holes in your network are only valuable if we evaluate your system as a whole. Many attacks focus on exploiting these vulnerabilities so they can gain access to your sensitive data while others are done to bring your whole network down.

Our review consists of more than the risks posed by your users but also against:

  • Hacks dealing with social engineering
  • Ransomware
  • Denial of Service Attacks (DoS)

Risk Management Framework/Asset Management and Tracking

With risk management framework, we make sure your organization’s information security is effective with the appropriate security controls. Each cyber security review we do will identify the data and information that is essential to your organization. Protecting all these assets is one of our most critical concerns when we develop a cyber security plan for you. Since every organization is different, we customize every program protection plan to suit your needs.

This includes:

  • Analyzing how to mitigate and prevent risks in your network
  • Identifying what information and assets are valuable
  • Evaluating your systems and controls that you already have in place
  • Identifying potential threats and vulnerabilities while assessing these risks.
